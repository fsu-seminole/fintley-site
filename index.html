Good questions

Is this a real appraisal?

No. Fintley gives you a fast estimate to screen deals quickly. For an offer you'd still want a licensed appraisal or a broker's opinion.

How accurate is the number?

Every estimate comes with a confidence level and a price range, so you can see how firm it is. The more recent sales nearby, the tighter that range gets.

Does it work for my area?

Any address in the continental US. Fintley reads the local market and prices the deal to match.

What does it cost?

Free to try, with three full reports a month. Pro adds unlimited searches and more. No account needed, and your reports stay on your device.
