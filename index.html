What's in a report

Run an address and here's what comes back.

Fintley's Forecast

One number from 1 to 100 that weighs price, cash flow, market momentum, and location. Anything above 90 is rare.

Value, with error bars

Sales comps and the income approach reconciled into a single estimate, with a margin that widens when the data disagrees.

Real cash flow

NOI, cap rate, cash-on-cash, and DSCR, built on live mortgage rates and itemized costs like actual taxes, HOA, and flood insurance.

Comparable sales

The five most similar nearby sales, each with a similarity score, so you can see exactly what the estimate rests on.

Market & neighborhood

12-month price trends, days on market, FEMA flood zones, and Census demographics for the ZIP.

Anywhere in the lower 48

Works for any continental-US address. Fintley figures out the market and prices the deal.
