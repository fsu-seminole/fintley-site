Six signals, one honest read on every property.

Fintley's Forecast

A calibrated 1–100 score blending price edge, cash flow, market momentum, and location — top scores are deliberately rare.

Honest valuation

Sales-comparison and income approaches reconciled into one number, with a ± error margin that widens when the data disagrees.

Real cash flow

NOI, cap rate, cash-on-cash, and DSCR — with live mortgage rates and itemized costs like real taxes, HOA, and flood-zone insurance.

Comparable sales

Five nearby comps with similarity scores, so the estimate is grounded in what actually sold.

Market & neighborhood

12-month price trends, days on market, FEMA flood risk, and Census demographics for the ZIP.
