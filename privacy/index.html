Privacy Policy

Effective June 7, 2026

Fintley is an iOS app that turns a US property address into an investment appraisal. The app doesn't have accounts or ads, and we never sell data. This page explains what the app sends to our server and what stays on your phone. It covers both the app and this website.

What we collect

We don't collect your name, email, location, contacts, or photos. Here is what the app does handle:

  • When you appraise a property, the address goes to our server, which forwards it to our data providers to pull property records, valuations, comparable sales, market statistics, demographics, and flood-zone data. Searches aren't linked to your identity.
  • On first launch the app generates a random device identifier. It isn't your Apple ID or your advertising ID, and it isn't tied to you. It rides along with search requests so we can enforce the free tier's monthly limit. Deleting the app deletes it; a reinstall generates a new one.
  • Your reports, watchlist, and notes live only on your device. We keep no copy, and deleting the app deletes them.
  • Fintley Pro purchases are handled entirely by Apple. We receive a signed receipt confirming the subscription is active, and we never see your payment details, billing address, or Apple ID email.

Our data providers

To build a report, our server queries a few third-party sources:

  • RentCast for property records, valuations, rent estimates, and comparable sales (their policy)
  • US Census Bureau for neighborhood demographics
  • FEMA for flood-zone maps
  • Federal Reserve (FRED) for current mortgage rates

They receive the address you searched, which is public information, and nothing that identifies you.

Analytics and ads

The app contains no analytics or advertising SDKs and no trackers. If we ever add anonymous usage stats, we'll update this policy first.

Data retention

Our server keeps short-lived operational logs, such as per-device search counts for quota enforcement, and doesn't maintain a history of which addresses you searched beyond what the quota requires. Everything else lives on your device.

Children

Fintley is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Your choices

  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data along with your anonymous identifier.
  • Manage or cancel Fintley Pro anytime in your Apple ID subscription settings.
  • Questions or requests: hello@fintley.app.

Changes to this policy

If we change how Fintley handles data, we'll update this page and the effective date above before the change takes effect.

Not financial advice

Fintley's estimates are for research. They aren't formal appraisals, and nothing in the app is financial, legal, or investment advice.
