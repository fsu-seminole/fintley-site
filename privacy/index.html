Privacy Policy

Effective June 7, 2026

The short version

Fintley has no accounts, no ads, no analytics trackers, and we never sell data. Searches are sent to our server anonymously so we can fetch property data; your reports, watchlist, and notes never leave your device.

Who we are

Fintley is an iOS app that turns a US property address into an investment appraisal. This policy describes what data the app handles and where it goes. It applies to the Fintley app and this website.

What we collect — and don't

We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, or photos. The app has no user accounts. What the app does handle:

  • Addresses you search. When you appraise a property, the address is sent to our server, which forwards it to our data providers to retrieve property records, valuations, comparable sales, market statistics, demographics, and flood-zone data. The address you search is not linked to your identity.
  • An anonymous device identifier. The app generates a random identifier on first launch (not your Apple ID, not your advertising ID, not tied to you). It is sent with search requests solely to enforce the free tier's monthly search limit and our API budget. Deleting the app deletes the identifier; reinstalling generates a new one.
  • Your reports, watchlist, and notes. Stored only on your device. We have no copy. Deleting the app deletes them.
  • Subscription status. Fintley Pro purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We receive a signed receipt confirming an active subscription; we never see your payment details, billing address, or Apple ID email.

Our data providers

To build a report, our server queries third-party sources, including:

  • RentCast — property records, valuations, rent estimates, comparable sales (their policy)
  • US Census Bureau — neighborhood demographics (public data)
  • FEMA — flood-zone data (public data)
  • Federal Reserve (FRED) — current mortgage rates (public data)

These providers receive the searched address (public information) but nothing that identifies you.

Analytics and ads

The app contains no third-party analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no trackers. If we ever add privacy-respecting, anonymous usage analytics, we will update this policy first.

Data retention

Our server keeps short-lived operational logs (such as per-device search counts for quota enforcement) and does not maintain a history of which addresses you searched tied to your device beyond what quota enforcement requires. Everything else lives on your device, under your control.

Children

Fintley is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Your choices

  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data and your anonymous identifier.
  • Manage or cancel Fintley Pro anytime in your Apple ID subscription settings.
  • Questions or requests: hello@fintley.app.

Changes to this policy

If we change how Fintley handles data, we'll update this page and the effective date above before the change takes effect.

Not financial advice

Fintley is a screening tool, not a formal appraisal, and its estimates are for research only — not financial, legal, or investment advice.
